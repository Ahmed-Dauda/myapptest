About Me

About the Author About the Author Muhammad Dagaci was born in Etsugaie in Agaie Local government of Niger state in September 1962. He attended Etsugaie Primary school, Government College, Bida, Government Science College, Kagara, School of basic studies (SBS) ABU, Zaria, Petroleum Training Institute (PTI), Warri and Ahamadu Bello University, Zaria. He holds a PTI Diploma in process plant operation and a Bachelor of Chemical Engineering. He is a writer whose numerous works have been published both by print (Leadership, Daily Trust, Liberator, news line newspapers) and social media. He belongs to the civil society family.

He presently chairs: Agaie Contact Group, NUPE-DEFO Association, Community Association for Grassroots Transformation (CAGRAT) and Patron Agaie Consultative Forum. He is also a member of the northern based elitist leading group-Gamji Development Association. The author was employed by the Department of Petroleum Resources in 1987. He has served at various locations including- Warri, Benin-city and Kaduna. He is a member of the Cooperative community of Nigeria and currently the President of the Staff Multi-Purpose Cooperative Society, DPR Kaduna. He is presently Head Depot Operations unit of the Kaduna Zonal office. Muhammad Dagaci who is widely travelled both within and outside the country is happily married with children.
